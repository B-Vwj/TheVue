## _Notes:_

#### Reciprocal identities:

- tan(x) = 1 / cot(x)

- sec(x) = 1 / cos(x)

- csc(x) = 1/ sin(x)

- tan(x) = sin(x) / cos(x)

- Cot(x) = cos(x) / sin(x)

#### Pythagorean Identities:

- Sin<sup>2</sup>(x) + cos<sup>2</sup>(x) = 1

- Tan<sup>2</sup>(x) + 1 = sec<sup>2</sup>(x)

- 1 + cot<sup>2</sup>(x) = csc<sup>2</sup>(x)

#### Odd-Even Identities:

- sin(-x) = -sin(x)

- tan(-x) = -tan(x)

- cot(-x) = -cot(x)

- csc(-x) = -csc(x)

—

- cos(-x) = cos(x)

- sec(-x) = sec(x)

#### Cofunction Identities:

- **cos(ø) = sin((π / 2) - ø)**

- **cot(ø) = tan((π / 2) - ø)**

- **csc(ø) = sec((π / 2) - ø)**
